AI Training

AI training for your team

You've invested in AI, but if your employees aren't sure how to use it, or which tools are even worth their time, that investment isn't doing much yet. And honestly, that's really common right now. Most people just haven't had anyone sit down with them to help it make sense to their workflow.

That's what we do. SLO Tech offers training programs for teams of every size and skill level, from the C-suite to the front desk. We keep it practical, approachable, and specific to the work your people are actually doing.

Lunch and Learns

A relaxed, one-hour session that gets your team curious and hands-on without eating up the workday. No homework, no jargon, just a good starting point.

Role-Based Workshop

A phone agent and a department head need completely different things from AI. We build each session around the real tasks your people do every day, so what they learn actually sticks.

Executive Briefings

For leadership teams who want to understand what they've invested in, how to drive adoption, and how to measure whether it's working.

Ongoing Coaching

AI moves fast and a one-time training only goes so far. We offer monthly check-ins and refreshers to keep your team growing with it.
